Chloe Atchue-Mamlet is a graphic design student and web enthusiast in Philadelphia, PA.

She loves design, writing, Netflix, and Amy Poehler, and is often the most eager person in the room.

About the Site

I coded this site myself as a way to start learning front-end development. Over the years I have tried to produce a portfolio site many times, but this is the first iteration that has actually made it to being public. I can't wait to keep improving the site over time. I used the wining combination of Jekyll and Github Pages, SASS as the CSS preprocessor, and Neat for the grid system. The typefaces are Alegreya Sans and Helvetica.
